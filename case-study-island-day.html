The Strategic Evolution

Converting event awareness into day-of attendance through behavioral design.

🎯

Direct Action Focus

Replaced generic "Register" language with urgent, high-value commands like **"Secure Your Spot,"** increasing the psychological commitment of the registrant.

🏙️

Brand Immersion

By leading with a prominent **Logo and Hero visual**, we reinforced the prestige of the institution before the student read a single word of the invite.

📱

Funnel Frictionless

Optimized the "Information Block" to be centered and high-contrast, ensuring that **mobile users**—who make up 70% of registrants—have zero friction.
